# 60-Second Demo Video Script

**Format:** Screen recording with voice-over. No face cam needed.
**Target platforms:** YouTube Shorts, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Product Hunt GIF
**Tone:** Calm, confident, no hype

---

## Shot List & Script

---

### [0:00 – 0:06] — Hook / Problem

**Screen:** Plain desktop. No app open yet.

**VO:**
> "You know you copied something — a link, a snippet, a phone number. But you can't find it."

**Screen:** Show `Win+V` → Windows clipboard history → user typing in search bar → no results found.

---

### [0:06 – 0:12] — Introduce Recall

**Screen:** App icon appears in system tray.

**VO:**
> "Recall remembers everything you copy — and lets you find it the way you actually remember it."

---

### [0:12 – 0:30] — Core Demo (the money shot)

**Screen:** Developer is working in VS Code. Switches to browser. Switches back.

**VO:**
> "Press Control-Shift-Space from anywhere."

**Screen:** Palette slides open over VS Code.

**VO:**
> "Type what you remember."

**Screen:** User types: `postgres connection string`

**Screen:** Results populate instantly. The right clip is at the top. It was copied 2 days ago.

**VO:**
> "Hit Enter."

**Screen:** Clip is pasted directly into the editor. Palette closes.

**VO:**
> "Done. Under three seconds."

---

### [0:30 – 0:42] — Feature highlights (fast cuts, no VO — let text labels do the work)

**Cut 1 (3s):** Auto-categorization panel — clips tagged as `code`, `url`, `email`. Label: *"Auto-categorized"*

**Cut 2 (3s):** User types `"that CSS trick"` — semantic result appears even though the clip text says `display: flex; gap: 8px;`. Label: *"Search by meaning, not exact text"*

**Cut 3 (3s):** Settings panel → AI section → shows "Local ONNX model — no API key". Label: *"Runs 100% on your machine"*

**Cut 4 (3s):** Pin shortcut (`Ctrl+P`) — clip moves to top with pin badge. Label: *"Pin the clips that matter"*

---

### [0:42 – 0:52] — Privacy / Trust moment

**Screen:** Settings → Privacy section. No telemetry toggles. All off.

**VO:**
> "Everything stays local. No cloud. No account. No telemetry. Your clipboard data is yours."

---

### [0:52 – 1:00] — CTA

**Screen:** GitHub repo page with star button visible.

**VO:**
> "Recall is free and open source. Windows and Linux. Link in the description."

**Screen:** End card — Recall logo + "github.com/piyushpradhan/smart-clipboard"

---

## Production Notes

- **Record at 1920×1080**, export at the native resolution — don't upscale
- **System font size:** bump to 125% before recording so text is legible at small playback sizes
- **Cursor:** use a cursor highlighter tool (e.g. Cursor Highlight for Windows, xdotool cursor effects on Linux)
- **Palette animation:** if the palette open/close isn't smooth enough in the recording, add a subtle ease-in via video editor
- **Music:** low-fi instrumental, -18dB relative to voice-over. Cut it entirely under the VO sections.
- **Captions:** add auto-captions for LinkedIn and X — most people watch muted
- **Aspect ratios:**
  - YouTube Shorts / TikTok: crop to 9:16 (show palette centered, cut side UI)
  - LinkedIn / X: 16:9 or 1:1
  - Product Hunt: export as GIF, 800px wide, 15fps, cover the core demo section (0:12–0:30) only

## Shorter Cuts

### 15-second cut (X/Twitter, LinkedIn ad)
Use [0:12 – 0:30] only. Add text overlay at start: "Clipboard manager with AI search." End with repo URL.

### 6-second GIF (Product Hunt thumbnail)
Capture just the palette open → type → result appears → Enter → pasted. Loop it.
